Question #05:
------------

The attacker checks the logging policies configured on the Win10 machine as part of the enumeration phase of their attack. What Windows "Living Off the Binary" do they use to check the audit policy?

ANSWER FORMAT: monkeyCTF{LivingOfftheLandBinaryNameWithExtension} (All lowercase. Filename ONLY, NOT full path.)
EXAMPLE ANSWER: moneyCTF{notepad.exe}

**IMPORTANT**
All relevent logs are between these time periods: Feb 24, 2022 @ 23:39:00.000 - Feb 25, 2022 @ 00:20:00.000   (EST)
It is important to note that the timezone above are in EST. If you machine is not in EST you will have to adjust the above times to your equivalent time zone.
You will know that you are looking at the correct period if there are 1,000,151 logs.
